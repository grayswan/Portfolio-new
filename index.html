#wastenot

This was my final project at The Iron Yard. It was completed in collaboration with a Ruby on Rails student as a potential non-profit organization aimed at reducing waste and helping the needy. The project required a way for participants to login and access their account as well as a way for new users to create an account. I designed and developed the entire front-end portion by hand using Sass, React, Backbone, jQuery, AJAX, and JSON.
